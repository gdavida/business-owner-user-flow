2
Initial Response
Timing: Days 1-60
Fast Track: Immediate Enthusiasm
Business owner sees page and immediately wants to enhance it
Proven Value: Wait & See
Business owner waits to see results before investing more time
Success Criteria: Engagement Signal
Page Sharing
Social media posts
Enhancement Interest
Clicks on upgrade prompts
Champion Feedback
Positive response
3
Value Realization
Timing: Weeks 2-12
Fast Track Path
Enhancement happens before results. Business owner invests based on enthusiasm and competitive comparison.
Proven Value Path
Results happen before enhancement. First customer attribution proves value, then business owner invests more time.
Success Criteria: Tangible Value
Customer Attribution
"Found you through directory"
Content Enhancement
Additional info added
Regular Check-ins
Views/updates their page

Active Participation
Timing: Month 3-12

Updates info, participates in campaigns, responds to prompts
Marketing Integration
Directory becomes part of regular marketing routine

Encourages other businesses to join
Success Criteria: Ongoing Engagement
Monthly Updates
Regular content refresh
Campaign Participation
Shop Local, seasonal
Peer Referrals
Brings in other businesses

Advocacy
Timing: Month 6+
